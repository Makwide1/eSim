FAQs

1. What is an eSIM? eSIM stands for Embedded SIM. It is a digital SIM card that is embedded in a device, like a smartphone or tablet. This means that there is no need for a physical SIM card, which can save space and make it easier for customers to switch service providers.

2. How does a customer benefit? • Smaller and more compact than physical SIM cards

• Easier to switch carriers.

• More secure

• Can be programmed remotely.

• You can add up to 5 eSIMs depending on the device capability.

3. What do I need to get an eSIM? Have a compatible device and Original National ID

4. Checking for device compatibility? Dial *#06#yes to confirm if the device has “EID” Examples of compatible devices
• iPhone XS and later
• Samsung Galaxy S20 and later (2020)
• Google Pixel 3 and later
• iPad Pro (2018) and later
• Apple Watch Series 3 and later

5. Can I switch my eSIM between devices? Yes

6. Can I switch from eSIM to a physical card? Yes

7. Is it possible to swap an eSIM? Yes
